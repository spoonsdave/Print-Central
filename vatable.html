IS IT VATABLE

VATABLE:

Stationery items: Letterheads, Comp Slips, Business Cards etc.

Stickers / Labels

Folders

Posters

Leaflets / Flyers – above 250gsm/over 25% area to be filled in (eg. Coupon)

Brochures (with pockets)

Cellobags

---

NON-VATABLE:

Brochures (without pockets)

Catalogues

Newsletters

Books

Leaflets/Flyers – under 250gms
